One pot lentil daal

This one pot lentil daal is a quick, easy and satisfying meal. Lentils are cheap, filling and healthy, and an excellent staple for those on a paleo or gluten free diet. The tumeric adds anti-inflammatory properties. Try it with a garnish of chives or sour cream.
